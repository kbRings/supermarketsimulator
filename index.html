About Supermarket Simulator

Supermarket Simulator is an engaging and educational game that offers players the chance to experience the challenges and rewards of managing a retail business. Created by a team of developers passionate about simulation games, this title brings the excitement of retail management to your screen.

Whether you're a casual gamer looking for a fun way to pass the time or someone interested in business management, Supermarket Simulator offers an accessible yet deep gameplay experience. The game combines realistic mechanics with engaging progression systems to keep you coming back for more.

Game Features

Realistic Simulation

Experience the authentic challenges of running a supermarket, from inventory management to customer service.

Business Expansion

Start small and grow your supermarket empire as you learn to optimize operations and increase profits.

Strategic Challenges

Face various challenges like rush hours, seasonal demands, and unexpected events that test your management skills.

Customer Relations

Build customer loyalty through excellent service, store layout optimization, and special promotions.

Staff Management

Hire, train, and manage employees to ensure your supermarket runs smoothly even during peak hours.

Customization Options

Design your supermarket layout, choose product selections, and create a unique shopping experience.

How To Play

Basic Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • E or Space: Interact with objects and customers
  • Tab: Open management menu
  • Esc: Pause game

Game Objectives

  • Stock Shelves: Purchase products and arrange them strategically
  • Serve Customers: Help customers find what they need
  • Manage Resources: Balance budget, staff, and inventory
  • Expand Business: Reinvest profits to grow your store
